# Lead Custom Fields

Lead Custom Fields attach structured, business-specific data to every lead. They appear in the Lead Detail drawer and are filled in by agents.

## Field Types

- **Text:** Free-form notes, names, URLs
- **Number:** Revenue figures, deal sizes, quantities
- **Dropdown:** Fixed choices (e.g., Starter / Pro / Enterprise)
- **Date:** Contract dates, follow-up deadlines
- **Yes / No:** Boolean flags (e.g., "Demo Completed?")

## Manage Custom Fields

### Add a Field
1. Open Settings > Lead Custom Fields.
2. Click Add Field.
3. Enter a name and choose a type.
4. For Dropdown, add your options.
5. Toggle Required if the field must always be filled.
6. Click Save Field.

![Add Fields](/img/add-field.JPG)

### Edit a Field
Click the pencil icon on any existing field to rename it, change its type, update dropdown options, or toggle the required flag.

### Delete a Field
Click the trash icon and confirm. This removes the field definition and all stored values for that field across all leads.

### Field Order
Fields display in the order they were created. To reorder, delete and re-create in the desired sequence.

## Examples

- Expected Revenue (Number)
- Product Interest (Dropdown: Starter, Pro, Enterprise)
- Industry (Text)
- Demo Completed (Yes / No)
- Contract Date (Date)
