# Settings

Settings is the configuration hub for your tenant. It's a grid of cards, each linking to one area of CallVibe you can configure.

![Settings page](/img/settings-overview.jpg)

## What You Can Access from Settings

- **Integrations:** Connect telephony providers, WhatsApp, and CRMs (GHL).
- **Notifications:** Configure Slack alerts and email digests.
- **Customer Management:** Tag customer numbers and control internal-number visibility in analytics.
- **AI Insights:** Configure call categories, custom AI extraction fields, and dropdowns.
- **Lead Statuses:** Define pipeline stages for lead management.
- **Lead Custom Fields:** Add structured fields (e.g., Expected Revenue, Product) to every lead.
- **Marketing Sources:** Manage custom lead-source labels for tenants without GHL connected.
- **Usage & Limits:** Monitor AI processing minutes against your plan's quota.

## Open Settings
1. Click Settings in the sidebar.
2. Click any card to open that section's configuration screen.

Each section is documented on its own page in this guide.
